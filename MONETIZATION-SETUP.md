# Errorfect Monetization Setup

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Digital Products (Low-Ticket: $10-30)
- **PDF Workbooks** - "10 Words That Quietly Control You"
- **Mini-Courses** - Recorded workshops, video series
- **Templates** - Word breakdown worksheets, journal prompts
- **Tools**: WooCommerce, Easy Digital Downloads, Gumroad integration

### 2. Live Workshops (Mid-Ticket: $50-150)
- **Group Sessions** - "Destroy 'Normal': Language Spellbreaking Lab"
- **Themed Workshops** - Perfectionism, Religious Trauma, Neurodivergence
- **Tools**: Event booking plugins, Zoom integration, payment processing

### 3. 1:1 Coaching (High-Ticket: $200-500/session)
- **Personalized Language Deprogramming**
- **Multi-session packages**
- **Tools**: Booking calendar, payment plans, client portal

### 4. Membership/Subscription (Recurring: $20-50/month)
- **Premium Content** - Advanced word breakdowns, exclusive workshops
- **Community Access** - Private forum, monthly Q&A
- **Tools**: MemberPress, Paid Memberships Pro, Restrict Content Pro

### 5. Affiliate/Partnerships
- **Book recommendations** (Amazon Associates)
- **Tool recommendations** (therapy apps, journaling tools)
- **Course partnerships** with other coaches/therapists

## Recommended Setup

### Phase 1: Quick Start (Week 1)
1. **Easy Digital Downloads** + **Stripe** - Sell PDFs immediately
2. **ConvertKit** - Build email list
3. **OptinMonster** - Capture leads with free PDF

### Phase 2: Workshops (Week 2-3)
1. **Calendly** integration - Book workshops
2. **Stripe** payment links - Collect workshop payments
3. **Zoom** integration - Host live sessions

### Phase 3: Membership (Month 2)
1. **MemberPress** - Create membership tiers
2. **Restrict Content** - Gated premium content
3. **Community Forum** - Add-on for members

## Pricing Strategy

## Conversion Funnel

1. **Awareness** - Blog posts, social media → Free PDF
2. **Interest** - Email sequence → Low-ticket product
3. **Consideration** - Workshop → Membership/Coaching
4. **Action** - High-ticket 1:1 or recurring membership
